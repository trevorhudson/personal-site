About

Hi! i'm Trevor. I'm originally from the central coast of california, but have lived in San Francisco for all of my adult life. I'm a software engineer and general-purpose nerd, with a particular interest on writing thoughtful, creative, and well-tested code.

I have a professional and academic background in Architecture, where my work centered on spatial planning and architectural design with an emphasis on sustainability, digital-fabrication, and computational modeling. I'm highly interested in the intersection of art and technology, and constantly looking for ways to leverage my background for future development projects.
